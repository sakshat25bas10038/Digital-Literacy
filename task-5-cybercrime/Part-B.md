## Stay safe online---Cyber Prevention checklist


### General Online Safety rules:


1.Always verify Person's email address or phone number before clicking onto any link.


2.Check whether there is "https:/" in the website before clicking so as to confirm whether it is a valid website.


3.Use strong and unique passwords to all your important accounts.


4.Dont forget to logout from your account while accessing it on someone else PC OR Mobile.


5 Use always the two step verification process for your any personal account.


### Financial UPI Safety:


1. Do not share your UPI Pin, OTP or any kind of bank details with any unknowing person.

   
2. Be cautious about" money collect" by entering UPI Pin. Remember you never receive money by entering you UPI Pin.


3. Verify 2 to 3 times receiver's account ID before sending him the money.

   
4. Do not connect to any public wifi when you are outside to avoid any such accidents.
