## Case Study: UPI Online Fraud with a Colledge Student.


### Whats this UPI Online Fraud?


It refers to deceptive activity where scammers exploit the UPI system to get the sensitive bank details for stealing there money.


### Scenario:


Kumar, a first year BBA student once received a call from a spammer asking for his UPI Pin so that 50000 rs can be added to his account .First, he refused it and was about to cut his call. But at the end moment the spammer made him to trust that he will actually receive the money and can confirm through an sms. Finally Kumar shared his UPI Pin with the spammer and received a message that your money has been debited to the person's account. Kumar realised soon as he cut the call that it was a fraud and he went to the police station to complain about it


### How it typically happens:


1. The Spammer uses fake but a convincing email ID or phone number.
2. He/she easily trick them by their strong communication power and convince them with quite ease.
3. Victim enters his personal accounts credentials or their UPI Pin or OTP.
4. Victim clicks on the fake link thus generating to a fake website.
5. The spammer further misuses these credentials and captures the data immediately.

### Who are mostly targetted?


Students,first time netbanking users, senior citizens and all those people who never made online transactions and dont know its rules are the most commonly tagetted people.


### Consequences:
1. Unauthorised access to our banking accounts and details
2. Mental and emotional stress to the people and distrust on the upi transactions
3. Financial loss through stolen credentials
